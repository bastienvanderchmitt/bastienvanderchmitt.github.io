About me

A very warm welcome to Ame de voyageur! My name is Zoe Hobson, a travel management graduate with a passion for travelling, design, learning and adventure. In November 2011 I booked a one-way ticket to Australia and have barely stopped travelling since! In January 2014, I embarked on a new journey following my creative instincts and this space was born! I created this space on the internet as a hub for people like me who want to be inspired to travel and live a more creative and adventurous life. For travel tips and to follow my current adventures, head over to my blog here

I am inspired by nature and by other creative people. My love for travel is the source of inspiration behind "Ame de voyageur" (Traveller’s Soul). Each country I visit gives more inspiration and new ideas. My little shop funds my dream to see the world.
